Dungeon Runner

  • The first project done by Rocket Punch Studios. This game started out as a college project which I then decided to continue work until it was feature complete. Dungeon Runner is an endless runner game with no true end. The goal is simple, get the high score. To achieve this you must overcome obstacles that are in your way. These obstacles include such things as walls, spikes, arrows, and skeletons. The obstacles can be overcome by swiping the correct way on the screen.
  • Release Date: May 31, 2016
  • Platform: Android
  • If you are interested in trying it out it is available for free on the Google Play store here.

Project: Roguelike

  • Project: Roguelike is an upcoming game currently under development. This game will take the player through a randomly generated dungeon with the goal of defeating the final boss. The main equipment for the game will be rings that will allow for passive stat bonuses as well as ways for the player to cast spells. While under development many features could be modified or changed completely. To follow the progress on the development of this game take at look at the blog page.
